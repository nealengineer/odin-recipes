Arrabiata

Description

Arrabiata is a fiery and flavorful Italian pasta sauce, aptly named "angry" for its bold and spicy kick. Made with tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and red chili peppers, the sauce tantalizes with a vibrant red color and enticing aroma. When paired with al dente pasta, it creates a harmonious balance of tangy sweetness and invigorating heat, making each bite a thrilling and unforgettable culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 small garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 1 x 400g can chopped tomatoes

Steps

  1. Pour the oil into a saucepan and fry the garlic over a medium heat for 1-2 mins until fragrant, but not browned.
  2. Add the chilli, fry for another minute, then add the tomatoes along with 1 tsp salt and Â½ tsp sugar.
  3. Stir well to combine the ingredients and simmer gently for 20 mins, stirring regularly.
  4. The sauce will thicken slowly as it cooks. Then, taste and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve stirred through pasta, or blend it first to create a smoother sauce if you prefer
